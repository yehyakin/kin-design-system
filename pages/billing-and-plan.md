# Billing and plan page

Status: draft

This page family is conditional. Use it only when a product exposes paid plans, metered usage, invoices, payment methods, tax information, cancellation, or payment recovery.

Before promotion, the contract must define:

- plan, entitlement, billing owner, billing period, renewal, currency, tax, and usage scope;
- current, upcoming, estimated, final, overdue, failed, refunded, disputed, and canceled states;
- upgrade, downgrade, proration, trial, renewal, cancellation, reactivation, and grace-period behavior;
- payment-provider handoff and return context;
- invoice availability, retention, and permissions;
- recent authentication and server authorization for sensitive changes;
- localization, mobile, accessibility, recovery, and support evidence.

KIN MUST NOT add pricing Cards, upgrade banners, or billing navigation to products without a real billing job.
