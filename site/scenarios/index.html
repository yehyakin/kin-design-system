Discovery is not adoption evidence.

Review the adoption boundary

Three statuses, one honest map

Source maturity describes the underlying KIN contract. Presentation status describes what this Atlas currently exposes. They are deliberately independent.

Showcased

A checked inspection route exposes only states implemented by its local reference, plus wide and narrow viewports and four theme modes.

Linked

A scenario may point to a runnable reference without claiming the checked controls required for showcased status.

Planned

The scenario is catalogued, but its dedicated Atlas presentation, states, or responsive review has not been completed.

Stable source

The governing page or product-pattern source is stable. This label does not make the Atlas presentation complete.

Candidate source

The source still has recorded gaps. Candidate and draft sources remain visible without being promoted.
